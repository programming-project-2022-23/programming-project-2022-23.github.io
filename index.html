Programming Project 2022-23

Welcome to the web page of the Programming Project course!

This a university course taught at the academic year 2022-23 for the Bachelor in Computer Science of the Free University of Bozen-Bolzano. Here, you will find information about the course, course material, and exercises.

Important dates:

  • First exam session:
    • Project submission: mid June
    • Project peer-review submission: End of June
    • Oral exam: Beginning of July
  • Second exam session:
    • Project submission: End of August
    • Project peer-review submission: Beginning of September
    • Oral exam: Mid September
